TAWI lifting equipment
Swedish engineering at its best

For decades, our lifting equipment has improved efficiency and employee health in businesses worldwide. Swedish quality and innovation has been the heart and soul of TAWI from the very start.

In Kungsbacka, a small town on the west coast of Sweden, TAWI head office is home to a dedicated staff, including everything from product development to production. Our team of experienced engineers design lifting solutions customised for your business needs. Their innovations are realised in the lifting equipment put together by skilled technicians, securing optimal quality and short lead times.

Our products are distributed through a worldwide network of distributors, and our own subsidiaries in the US and Canada, UK, Germany, France, Spain, Netherlands, Denmark, Sweden and China.

Service and support

Dedicated customer support is guaranteed when investing in TAWI lifting solutions and our service team will assure that the equipment never fails you. Choosing TAWI means that you get Swedish quality and reliability, in product, service and support. We offer a wide selection of lifting solutions for everything from food manufacturing to automotive industry.

 

Complete supplier

Our lifting equipment handles everything from boxes and sacks, to cheese and windshields. We can turn, tilt and rotate heavy barrels, large panels and much more. Our solutions include vacuum lifters, electric lifters, hoists, crane systems and mobile lifters. This means that TAWI equipment cover all your lifting needs.
