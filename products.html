Lifting devices for a productive and ergonomic workplace

Through innovative Swedish engineering, TAWI material handling equipment help create a work environment where everyone can lift, increasing productivity and employee health. Easy lifting is the key to safe and efficient material handling, no matter what you need to lift.

Industrial equipment for easy lifting

Most manufacturing industries, logistic centres, and other businesses handling lightweight goods have a lot to gain from implementing some sort of lifting device in their workflow. Manufacturing industries often use TAWI Lifting Trolleys to mount reels in packaging machines, or to transport ready products from the production line. Vacuum lifters and hoists are frequently used for lifting raw material like wood or metal, food ingredients or chemicals. They are also great for the high frequency lifts that often occur at the end of a production line, or when packing products on pallets. TAWI lifting equipment help ensure ergonomic lifting and productivity in workplaces all over the world.

Experienced vacuum lifter manufacturer

TAWI has developed and manufactured vacuum lift systems for decades. With their combined vacuum liftig expertise, the TAWI team will make sure that you get the lift system best suited for your needs.

User-friendly and customized lifters

All TAWI lifting devices are developed with the user in mind, designed to make lifting easy for everyone, no matter where they work. We modify our products for customers, ensuring that your TAWI lifter will always meet your needs, increasing productivity in your business. Specific industry requirements and individual operator needs are considered in the development, making sure the device will be easy to use and will not interfere with any requirements set on your business.

Complete lift systems

Combined with TAWI crane systems, our vacuum assisted lifting devices make a complete vacuum lifting system, easily integrated in your business. In addition we also supply easy to use lifting trolleys and hoist lifts that can lift pretty much anything. As a complete supplier, TAWI will be the only partner you need when it comes to efficient and ergonomic lifting of lightweight goods.
