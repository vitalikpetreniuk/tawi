Wood industry packaging challenges

Zabolak specialize in finishing of wooden flooring and panels, from raw material to structuring, staining, oiling and packaging. After packaging, the finished wood bundles need to be palletized. The bundles weigh up to 50kg and without ergonomic lifting equipment this needs to be handled by 2 people.

Palletizing 50kg bundles of wood without effort

At Zabolak in the Netherlands, palletizing the finished wood product is facilitated by using TAWI vacuum lifter. The operators use the lift system on a daily basis and it makes the work easier and improves employee health. Using the vacuum lifter, the physical effort needed to put the large bundles on pallets is minimized.

Improved workforce flexibility

According to Mr. Henny Jacobs, Managing Director at Zabolak in Netherlands, another advantage of using TAWI lifting equipment is that every employee can work with the lifter, even temporary personnel learns very easy how to work with the lifter. “This makes people very flexible on that work station”, Henny Jacobs, Zabolak.
